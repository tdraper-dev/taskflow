# Summer Peach Basil Bourbon Smash 🍑🌿

The ultimate refreshing cocktail that combines the sweetness of fresh summer peaches with aromatic basil and smooth bourbon. This crowd-pleasing drink is guaranteed to impress your neighborhood friends at any summer gathering!

## Ingredients

### For One Cocktail:
- 2 oz bourbon whiskey (preferably a smooth variety like Maker's Mark or Woodford Reserve)
- 1 ripe peach, pitted and cut into wedges
- 6-8 fresh basil leaves
- 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
- 1/2 oz simple syrup (adjust to taste based on peach sweetness)
- 2-3 oz club soda
- Ice cubes
- Pinch of sea salt (optional, but recommended)

### For Simple Syrup:
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup water

### Garnish:
- Fresh basil sprig
- Peach slice
- Lemon twist

## Instructions

### Making Simple Syrup (if needed):
1. **Combine**: In a small saucepan, combine equal parts sugar and water.
2. **Heat**: Bring to a simmer, stirring until sugar dissolves completely.
3. **Cool**: Remove from heat and let cool. Store in refrigerator for up to 1 month.

### Preparing the Cocktail:

1. **Muddle the fruit and herbs**: In a cocktail shaker, gently muddle the peach wedges and basil leaves. Don't over-muddle the basil - you want to release the oils without making it bitter.

2. **Add the bourbon**: Pour in the bourbon and let it sit for 30 seconds to infuse with the muddled ingredients.

3. **Add citrus and sweetener**: Add fresh lemon juice, simple syrup, and a pinch of sea salt if using.

4. **Shake with ice**: Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds until well chilled.

5. **Double strain**: Strain through a fine-mesh strainer into a rocks glass filled with fresh ice to remove pulp and basil pieces.

6. **Top and garnish**: Top with club soda and gently stir. Garnish with a fresh basil sprig, peach slice, and lemon twist.

## Pro Tips for Maximum Impressiveness

- **Peach perfection**: Use the ripest peaches you can find. They should yield slightly to gentle pressure and smell fragrant.
- **Fresh herbs**: Always use fresh basil - dried won't give you the same aromatic punch.
- **Ice matters**: Use large, clear ice cubes if possible. They melt slower and won't dilute your masterpiece.
- **Presentation**: Serve in a nice rocks glass or mason jar for that rustic summer vibe.
- **Make ahead**: You can prepare the muddled peach-basil mixture hours ahead and store in the fridge.

## Batch Recipe (Serves 8)

Perfect for entertaining! Scale up for your neighborhood party:

- 16 oz bourbon
- 8 ripe peaches, muddled
- 48-64 fresh basil leaves
- 6 oz fresh lemon juice
- 4 oz simple syrup
- 16-24 oz club soda
- Plenty of ice

Combine all ingredients except club soda in a large pitcher. Stir well and chill. When serving, pour over ice and top with club soda.

## Variations to Try

- **Spicy Kick**: Add a thin jalapeño slice while muddling
- **Smoky Version**: Use a peated bourbon or add a splash of mezcal
- **Virgin Version**: Replace bourbon with peach nectar and ginger beer
- **Frozen**: Blend with ice for a slushy summer treat

## Perfect Pairings

This cocktail pairs beautifully with:
- Grilled chicken or pork
- Fresh summer salads
- Cheese boards with aged cheddar
- BBQ dishes
- Peach cobbler (obviously!)
